Insights Industry observations
Performance Gap
Green certification is lagging behind actual building performance
Many LEED-certified buildings fail to meet modelled energy targets once occupied. The gap between design intent and operational performance is one of the field's most under-discussed problems — and a core reason I moved toward energy simulation.
Tools
Ladybug + Honeybee is changing how architects engage with climate data
Parametric environmental analysis is no longer just for engineers. Tools like Ladybug and Honeybee bring solar, wind, and thermal analysis directly into the design environment — closing the loop between geometry and performance.
Practice
Large firms are under-investing in performance literacy among architects
Across AECOM, Nikken, and Atkins, energy decisions were largely delegated to MEP consultants. Architects who can speak fluently across both disciplines will become increasingly essential as net-zero targets tighten.
